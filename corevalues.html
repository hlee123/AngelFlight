CORE VALUES


Our Core Values Keep Us Centered and Focused


At Angel Flight Marketing Services, our core values affect and influence everything we do. They are the standards by we measure our performance and judge our interactions with both staff and clients.


Our core values include:


  • Integrity
  • Honesty
  • Commitment to excellence
  • Respect to our employees and clients
  • Honor divergent points of view
  • Nurture ideas, creativity and innovation

We strive for:


  • Continual self-improvement
  • High-quality creative outputs
  • Accurate and verifiable results
  • Increased productivity
  • Market discipline
  • Freedom of expression
  • Appreciation of team members and clients

Finally, we respect all laws and regulations (statewide, regionally and nationally) that directly extends Angel Flight Services' ethics and values.
